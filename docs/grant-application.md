# IOTA GasKit Grant Application Draft

## One-line pitch

IOTA GasKit is an open-source toolkit that helps IOTA dApp builders deploy, secure, monitor, and integrate gas-sponsored transactions using the official IOTA Gas Station component.

## What we are building

The project delivers a reusable production-readiness layer around IOTA Gas Station: deployment templates, a policy and quota gateway, a TypeScript SDK, integration examples, an operator dashboard, observability templates, and hardening documentation.

## Why it matters

Gasless onboarding removes token-acquisition friction for end users. IOTA has the sponsored-transaction primitive, but teams still need safe deployment, quotas, usage tracking, monitoring, and examples. GasKit turns that repeated operational work into shared open-source infrastructure.

## Grant scope

The grant funds the open-source toolkit. It does not fund a closed managed SaaS. Future hosted services may provide operations and support, while the toolkit remains independently deployable.

## Recommended ask

$49,000 across five milestones. See `docs/grant-milestones.md`.
