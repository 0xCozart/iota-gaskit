# Grant Scope

The IOTA GasKit grant funds open-source ecosystem infrastructure.

## In scope

- local deployment kit;
- policy gateway and quotas;
- TypeScript SDK;
- example integrations;
- operator dashboard;
- observability templates;
- threat model and production hardening docs;
- grant demo and reviewer artifacts.

## Out of scope for grant MVP

- hosted SaaS billing;
- commercial SLAs;
- enterprise SSO;
- legal/compliance services;
- generalized cross-chain sponsorship;
- custody of user funds;
- proprietary managed hosting features.

A future managed service may be built around hosting and support, but the toolkit must remain independently useful.
